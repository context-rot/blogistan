## 🧬 About

**Context Rot** explores the inevitable entropy of context in AI systems through ArXiv-style preprints that combine rigorous analysis with appropriate academic irreverence.

We investigate how context degrades, evolves, and transforms in computational systems—from language models losing coherence over long conversations to knowledge bases becoming stale and contradictory.

> *"The only constant in AI is decay. We're here to document it with appropriate academic gravitas and tongue-in-cheek commentary."*

---

## 🔬 Research Focus Areas

### **Context Window Decay**
- Long-range dependency degradation in transformer architectures
- Memory consolidation strategies for extended conversations
- Attention mechanism entropy analysis

### **Knowledge Base Erosion** 
- Information freshness metrics and decay curves
- Conflicting knowledge resolution in large models
- Dynamic knowledge graph evolution

### **Computational Entropy**
- Measuring information loss in neural network compression
- Gradient decay patterns during training
- Model capability regression over time

### **Interactive Research Environment**
- Line-by-line feedback systems for academic papers
- Community-driven error correction and insights
- AI-assisted research improvement workflows

## 🚀 Getting Started

### For Readers
1. Visit [context-rot.com](https://context-rot.com)
2. Browse our latest publications
3. Select any text to leave comments and feedback
4. Join the conversation about computational decay

### For Contributors
1. **Fork** the [blogistan repository](https://github.com/context-rot/blogistan)
2. **Clone** your fork locally
3. **Install** Jekyll and dependencies: `bundle install`
4. **Run** locally: `bundle exec jekyll serve`
5. **Submit** pull requests with improvements

### For Researchers
- Submit papers via GitHub issues with the `paper-submission` label
- Follow our [ArXiv-style formatting guidelines](https://context-rot.com/guidelines)
- Engage with the community through our interactive commenting system

## 🌟 Community Guidelines

### **Academic Rigor with Levity**
We maintain scientific standards while embracing the absurdity of our field. Expect:
- **Peer-reviewed sarcasm** 
- **Methodologically sound satire**
- **Evidence-based pessimism**
- **Reproducible cynicism**

### **Contribution Standards**
- **Be respectful** in all interactions
- **Provide citations** for claims and references
- **Embrace constructive criticism** as part of the process
- **Maintain the balance** between serious research and academic humor
